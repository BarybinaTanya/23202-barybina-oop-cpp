Hello! It's the Life Game! Here are rules of the game:

1 File Format:

1.1 The game uses Life 1.06 input file format. It means that if you want to initialise specific cells alive, you
need to write their coordinates first. Coordinate of the cell here is the shift of this cell to x positions
by coordinate x and y positions by coordinate y. Shifts may also have negative values. Example:
0 -1
1 0
-1 1
0 1
1 1
